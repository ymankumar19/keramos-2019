A hundred years. For an institute of national importance to achieve the glory of celebrating the completion of 100 years is a remarkable feet itself. It gives us immense pride to be a part of the physical manifestation of the vision of our founder, Bharat Ratna Mahamana Pandit Madan Mohan Malviya Ji. The journey from the erstwhile technical colleges of BHU, viz, BENCO, MINMET and TECHNO to IT(BHU) and since 2012, IIT(BHU)Varanasi has been a journey of growth, learning and most importantly, character building. On occasion of centenary completion, we would like to express our deep gratitude for the strenuous efforts from the great men and women for development of an indigenous autonomous university. The institute has the credit of first starting degree classes in Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Metallurgy and Pharmaceutics. Today, IIT BHU consists of 10 engineering departments and 3 science departments and 3 inter-disciplinary schools. It also boasts of a vast and strong alumni network spread all across the globe and in various fields. IIT (BHU) harbors a wonderful environment with a vibrant history, a magnanimous alumni network & a beautiful campus. By producing a line of competent, courteous & well-bred individuals, the institute clearly justifies its motto of "Education is character."
